THE SYSTEM

Four steps. One closed loop.

☀️

Generate

Solar panels, wind turbines, or hydro produce real, measurable kilowatt-hours of electricity.

→
🪙

Mint

For every 1 kWh measured, 1 Energy Token is created and recorded on a public ledger.

→
👥

Distribute

Tokens are split equally among all participants — automatically, on a set schedule.

→
🛒

Spend

Use tokens for electricity, local goods, services, or exchange them for fiat currency.

♻️ The cycle repeats — every single day, forever.

🌍 Real UBI Pilots That Already Worked
🇫🇮
Finland — 2017–2018
2,000 unemployed citizens received €560/month, no conditions.
✅ Higher wellbeing, less stress, more trust in institutions. Employment stable — disproving the "people stop working" myth.
🇺🇸
Stockton, CA — SEED 2019–2021
125 low-income residents received $500/month for 2 years.
✅ Full-time employment doubled (28% → 40%). Mental health improved significantly. Financial stability increased.
🇺🇸
Alaska Permanent Fund — 1982–now
Every Alaskan resident receives $1,000–$2,000/year from oil revenues.
✅ Alaska has had one of the lowest poverty rates in the US for 40+ years. No negative effects on workforce participation.
🇰🇪
Kenya — GiveDirectly (ongoing)
Long-term UBI study across 197 villages, 20,000+ recipients.
✅ Increased spending on food, health & assets. Work hours went UP — people invest in themselves and their communities.
